# Q&A

### What is the purpose?
The purpose is to create a community of members to become inspired or help develop other projects across the browser. It can allow for multiple group projects to be developed on our website and have a place where members can share their ideas to the world. 

### What are the benefits of the website?
The benefits include a place where multiple people can work on one project, members can see what other creators are developing, and it is an organized site where members can come together to distriute ideas. 

### What are the basics of the website?
As you go to the website, you will be able to create an account/login and with that access you can then upload/view other projects that are shared to the website during that time.
